Hi! My name is Gabriel (Buo-Xuan) Huang and I am doing a PhD in machine learning, at the Montreal Institute for Learning Algorithms (MILA). My supervisor is Simon Lacoste-Julien.

I am interested in latent-variable models, structured prediction, weakly-supervised learning, reinforcement learning, convex optimization, music generation, and fundamental questions of optimization and statistical learning.

Previously I did the MVA Master's degree in machine learning at École Normale Supérieure, in parallel with an engineer's degree at École Centrale Paris (now CentraleSupélec). While I was doing my master's, I worked two days every week at Dassault Systèmes to do research on computer vision. Before that, I did classe préparatoire at Lycée Hoche in France.
